Want to be featured?

We are always looking for content to share with our community. If you have a piece of art, work, or anything Geography related that you'd like to share with us, feel free to shoot us an email at plenum@uw.edu or DM us on any of our social media platforms. Submissions of this nature have no strict submission criteria or formatting. Some examples of content might include:

  • Creative writing (e.g. poems, short stories)
  • Maps or other data visualizations
  • Pictures of unique or interesting Geographies
  • Short essays or news summaries
  • Almost anything Geography related!

Your work will be shared with the Geography undergraduate community throughout our various social media platforms including email, Instagram, Twitter , or LinkedIn. You will be tagged and appropriately credited. Reach out by email or DM'ing us on any of these platforms!
